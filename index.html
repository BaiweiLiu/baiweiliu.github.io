About me

I am a researcher in cognitive neuroscience, currently working as a Postdoctoral Fellow at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. I will formally receive my PhD diploma later this year. As my current contract is ending soon, I am actively seeking new research opportunities starting early next year.

My research broadly investigates how neural system supports behavior. In particular, I am interested in the flow of information—how behaviorally relevant inputs are perceived, represented, processed, and finally become or guide our behavior.
